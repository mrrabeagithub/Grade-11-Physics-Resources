The Rotating Crane Arm

Grade 11 Scientific | Chapter 10: Rotational Dynamics

Problem Context & Given Data

A homogeneous rigid rod OB of mass $M=3\text{ kg}$ and length $L=2\text{ m}$ can rotate without friction in a vertical plane around a fixed horizontal axis ($\Delta$) passing through its extremity O.

  • Gravity: $g=10\text{ m/s}^{2}$
  • Moment of Inertia (Rod at end): $I_{rod}=\frac{1}{3}ML^{2}$
  • Sign Convention: Counter-clockwise (CCW) is the positive $(+)$ direction of rotation.
